Meet Kelvin: Your Boxing & Mixed Martial Arts Expert

Kelvin is a dynamic trainer with extensive experience in boxing and mixed martial arts (MMA). His training combines discipline, technique, and intensity to help clients build strength, improve agility, and gain self-defense skills.

Training Philosophy

Kelvin’s approach focuses on mental and physical resilience. Boxing and MMA are about building confidence, discipline, and a strong connection between mind and body. His training will help you develop razor-sharp focus and push your limits.

Training Methods

Boxing: Master the Art of the Sweet Science

  • Footwork & Movement: Improve balance, agility, and defensive skills.
  • Punching Techniques: Master jabs, uppercuts, and striking with accuracy.
  • Pad Work & Heavy Bag Training: Coordination and endurance drills.
  • Conditioning: Shadowboxing and jump rope for cardio fitness.

Mixed Martial Arts (MMA): A Full-Body Combat Sport

  • Striking: Learn punches, kicks, elbows, and knees with precision.
  • Grappling & Takedowns: Master wrestling and grappling techniques.
  • Submission Techniques: Fundamentals of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.
  • Sparring: Practice controlled sparring in a safe environment.

Functional Fitness for Combat Athletes

Strength and conditioning exercises tailored for combat sports to improve performance in the ring or cage.

Why Train with Kelvin?

  • Comprehensive Training: A program combining technical skills, endurance, and strength.
  • Self-Defense Skills: Gain confidence to protect yourself in real-life situations.
  • Tailored Programs: Customized sessions based on your goals.
  • Empowering Environment: Kelvin creates a motivating, supportive atmosphere.
