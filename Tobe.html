Meet Tobe: Your calisthenics athlete and a trained Yoga Teacher

Bimarsha is a passionate and highly skilled trainer who combines weight training and endurance techniques to help individuals reach their fitness goals. With years of experience, Bimarsha tailors every program to suit all fitness levels, ensuring you build strength, stamina, and confidence.

Training Philosophy

Tobe believes in a holistic approach that combines the strength and flexibility of calisthenics with the mindfulness and balance of yoga. This integration not only enhances physical fitness but also promotes mental well-being.

Training Methods

Calisthenics

Tobe focuses on bodyweight exercises that develop functional strength, endurance, and muscle definition. His sessions include movements like push-ups, pull-ups, dips, and squats, tailored to individual fitness levels. Progressive overload is a key principle in his training, ensuring continuous improvement by gradually increasing exercise intensity.

Yoga

Incorporating yoga into his routine, Tobe emphasizes flexibility, mobility, and mental clarity. His yoga sessions involve dynamic flows and static postures, enhancing joint health and reducing the risk of injury. This combination of practices leads to a balanced and sustainable fitness regimen.

Training Programs

Tobe offers personalized training programs that blend calisthenics and yoga, suitable for all fitness levels. Whether you're beginning your fitness journey or aiming to enhance your current routine, Tobe's programs are designed to help you achieve your goals through consistent practice and dedication.
